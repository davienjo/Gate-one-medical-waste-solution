About Us

We are Nairobi-based medical waste experts, providing safe, reliable, and eco-friendly collection, transportation, and disposal of healthcare waste. From sharps to pharmaceuticals and chemicals, we ensure every step follows strict safety and environmental standards.

Mission: Protecting people, communities, and the environment through safe and compliant medical waste solutions.

Values: Safety • Compliance • Eco-Responsibility • Reliability • Integrity

Our Services

Gate One’s dedicated team is committed to providing safe, reliable, and eco-friendly medical waste solutions for healthcare facilities. Our services include:

Healthcare Waste Collection

Safe removal of all types of medical waste from clinics, hospitals, and labs.

Pharmaceutical Waste Management

Secure disposal of expired or unused medicines.

Chemical Waste Disposal

Secure disposal of expired or unused chemicals and hazardous materials.

Transportation of Medical Waste

Safe and compliant transport of medical waste from your facility to our disposal sites.

We also provide a range of high-quality medical waste products, from sharps boxes to color-coded waste bags, designed to make safe disposal simple, reliable, and compliant.
